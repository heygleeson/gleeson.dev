What is this page?

This page is inspired by the concept of a /now/ page — a page that tells you what this person is focused on at this point in their life. This is to differentiate from things like an About page or a Contact page, which are much more commonplace and are typically static.

I have taken the basic idea and created a tiny micro-blog. For me, this page serves as a way to update this site without committing to longer blog posts.
